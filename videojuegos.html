Earth's Twilight

This game is a Sci-Fi FPS developed for a University class between only 2 people.The project took 2 months of planning and 2 months of development, during which my teammate and I focused on the mechanics and dynamics, and saved time on the visual part by using 3D assets purchased from the Unity Asset Store.

My role in the project focussed on developing the main mechanics, post-processing, narrative, UX and another areas.
